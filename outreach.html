Outreach

We as a team have gained over 200+ hours of outreach through hosting information sessions, workshops, Intro To Robotics meetings, and mock tournaments for FLL. We also donated old FLL boards to new teams in our community and helped create new teams through these meetings and the support of our coaches.

Our team always strives to help others access this wonderful program, and we look forward to doing so every season.
