Our Sponsors

RAY

RAY is a non-profit organization founded by our mentors from a previous FTC team, the Bionic Enforcers. They provide young kids opportunities to learn robotics and have helped cover many of our team costs.

GoBilda

GoBilda creates starter kits for robots. Their starter kit helped us kick off our FTC journey and build our first robot.

Swartek

Swartek is a tech company specializing in Data & AI, Analytics, and Cloud Solutions. They provided $500 for our team, which we used to create new team t-shirts.

Polymaker

Polymaker develops 3D printing materials. They provided $200 worth of filament, helping us create 3D pieces for our robot.
